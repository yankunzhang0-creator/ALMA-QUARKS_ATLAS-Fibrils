# ALMA-QUARKS_ATLAS-Fibrils
### The PDF file is the ATLAS of the fibrils found in the ALMA-QUARKS survey. Its corresponding paper is comming soon. Please wait.

### Below are the description of the ATLAS:

##### Backgrounds -- the 1.3-mm continuum emission detected in the 139 proto-clusters;
##### Sky-blue and light-grey curves -- the spines and branches of the skeletons in fibrils, respectively;
##### The blue and yellow numbers -- the indexing of fibrils processed in RadFil and FilFinder;
##### Limegreen contours -- the H40α emission detected in the ATOMS survey; 
##### purple circles -- cores found in Jiao et al. (in preparation).
